This blog is about my journey through the complexities of Malware Analysis and Cyber Threat Intelligence (CTI).

One of the reasons I enjoy cybersecurity so much is my genuine passion for learning. I have always been curious as to why or how something works never accepting "it just works". With malware I am not only able to satisfy this hunger but I am also able to translate my findings into actionable intelligence. Personally every sample analyzed is an opportunity to expand my understanding of the constantly shifting threat landscape while providing positive impact.

Impact is something I also find infinitely valuable; when I was a blue teamer I felt immense fulfillment knowing I was defending the individuals that my company at the time directly affected. This is multiplied in my current role as a red teamer as I am now able to impact hundreds of companies that involve thousands if not millions of people.

By documenting my research into various malware families and exploitation techniques here, I hope to provide further impact to the community while refining my own expertise.
